# LinkedIn Content Strategy

> **Goal:** Position Victor Palma Carrasco as a thought leader in Android, full-stack, and AI engineering.
> **Rhythm:** 1-2 posts per week · Tuesday–Thursday · 8–10am CET
> **Niche:** "Senior engineer who builds real things across mobile, web, and AI — and shares what he learns."

---

## Post Format Rules

- **First line is everything** — LinkedIn shows only the first line before "see more". Make it a hook, not a title.
- **Short paragraphs** — one idea per line, no walls of text.
- **End with a question** — drives comments, which boost reach dramatically.
- **Max 3 hashtags** — `#Android` `#SoftwareEngineering` `#AI` — no spam.
- **Post Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am CET** — peak LinkedIn engagement for tech.

---

## Hook Formula

```
[Surprising statement or number]
[1 line of context]
[List or story]
[Closing question]
```
