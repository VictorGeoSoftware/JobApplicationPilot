> Prepared for **Victor Palma Carrasco** — Senior Software Engineer (Android/Kotlin, Node.js backend, AI-augmented dev, blockchain) · Surveyor Engineering degree · Based in **Uddevalla, Sweden** · English fluent / Spanish native / Swedish basic.

## Why drones are a strong fit for your profile

- **Surveyor Engineering degree + software** is the textbook bridge into **drone mapping / geospatial / photogrammetry** platforms. You understand both the domain (surveying) and the code.
- **Android / Jetpack Compose** maps directly to **ground-control station (GCS) and field apps** that almost every drone company ships.
- **Node.js / backend + cloud** maps to **fleet management, telemetry ingestion, and data-processing pipelines**.
- **AI-augmented engineering** is highly relevant to the current European **autonomy + defense-tech** funding wave.
- Best-fit role categories for you (in priority order):
  1. **Mobile / GCS app engineer** (Android Compose)
  2. **Backend / cloud / data-platform engineer** (fleet, telemetry, geospatial)
  3. **Autonomy tooling / AI-assisted engineering**
